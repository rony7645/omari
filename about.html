About Shepherd Park Terrace

The Shepherd Park Terrace Civic Club's purpose is to assist in enforcing all deed restrictions, oversee maintenance, and advocate for neighborhood functions and improvements conducive to the progress of the community and the well-being of its residents.

It is an entirely volunteer-based group and is a 501(c)(4) non-profit tax-exempt organization. Our community is thriving after decades of sustained growth, safety, and a shared sense of pride in our collective neighborhood.
